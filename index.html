About Us

Samvaad, The Literature And Quizzing Society of PDPM IIITDM Jabalpur, is an open convention for all the thoughtful minds having love for literature. The club is committed to tap the effective communication talent among the students and is poised to enable the talents with a cutting edge. To develop and hone the literary skills of the students, club inspires the students to develop a taste for literature and also works in the direction of educating their spoken and written language. It is intricate in a variety of activities aimed at building up the confidence and grooming the talents of students in facing various interpersonal activities and competitions. Samvaad is host to many events and sessions, namely Debate, Group Discussions, Quiz, Spell Bee, Poetry, Creative Writing, JAM, MUN, Elocution, Extempore, Anchoring and many more. We expand our overview to shed light on the issues that often over looked but affect our lives deeply. We believe in providing an opportunity to everyone so all the voices are heard. We believe in discussing the various issues that concern us. We believe we can make a change, with our words and subsequently with our actions.

Club Policy

  1. All the members should be officially registered with Samvaad – The Literature and Quizzing Society.

  2. The registration process will be carried forward through a photo attested membership form to be filled in by the students wishing to become part of Samvaad – The Literature and Quizzing Society.

  3. Only registered members of Samvaad – The Literature and Quizzing Society will be considered for any position in the The Literature and Quizzing Working Committee. The Literature and Quizzing Working Committee is a student body comprising of active second year and third year members of Samvaad – The Literature and Quizzing Society. The decision regarding the sessions, budget, events, workshops, seminars and any other matter concerning Samvaad – The Literature and Quizzing Society is resolved after consideration of the majority in the meeting of The Literature and Quizzing Working Committee. Each member of The Literature and Quizzing Working Committee is given responsibility of particular events.

  4. Apart from competitions (open to all), only the registered members of Samvaad – The Literature and Quizzing Society shall be considered for any public speaking event under the responsibility of Samvaad – The Literature and Quizzing Society.

Fests

Apart from the sessions and events Samvaad also organises different Fests throughout
the year which makes it the most active club of IIITDM Jabalpur

Fiesta Da Literati

Fiesta-da-Literati, intra-college Literature and Quizzing Festival, is the first of the cultural festivals for the odd semesters, conducted in the month of September. It brings in much needed excitement after the mid semester examinations and sees huge participation from students of the institute.

Alfaaz

Alfaaz, inter-college Literature and Quizzing Festival, is the first to be conducted for the even semesters, in the month of January. It witnesses a true spectrum of talent when students from far and beyond come to participate in this ever exciting festival.
